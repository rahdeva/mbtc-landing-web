Your Journey Starts Here

Nestled in the heart of Bali's enchanting landscape, Magical Bali Toastmasters empowers members to develop communication and leadership skills in an environment as magical as our island home.

We believe that everyone has a story worth telling and a voice worth hearing. Whether you're a beginner looking to overcome stage fright or an experienced speaker aiming to refine your craft, you'll find your place among fellow growth-seekers.

Our supportive community combines structured learning with the warmth of Balinese hospitality, creating an atmosphere where transformation happens naturally.

Weekly Structure

Our Meeting Agenda

Join us for our Regular Meeting on the 1st and 3rd Friday of every month. A structured yet welcoming session designed to maximize your growth as a communicator.

7:00 PM

Welcome & Networking

Mingle with fellow members and guests. Enjoy light refreshments and build meaningful connections in our vibrant community.

7:15 PM

Opening & Introductions

The Toastmaster of the Evening opens the meeting and introduces the evening's theme, setting the stage for inspiration.

7:30 PM

Prepared Speeches

Members deliver speeches from the Pathways program, showcasing their progress and demonstrating advanced communication techniques.

8:00 PM

Table Topics

Impromptu speaking session to develop quick thinking and spontaneous communication skills. Perfect for building confidence.

8:20 PM

Evaluations

Constructive feedback on speeches, helping members grow through supportive and actionable insights.

8:45 PM

Closing & Awards

Announce Best Speaker, Best Evaluator, and Best Table Topics awards. Celebrate achievements and share closing remarks.
